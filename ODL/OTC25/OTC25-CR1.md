# Introduction
This Cr-2 id designed to manage the datasets collected during the OTC25 so that they can be fully exploited by the students and staff that participated in the OTC25 Voyage.

The aim of the work is:
*TO Archive, pre-process and manage the datasets collected during the ESA OTC25 for further analysis by the OTC25 participants.*

The aim of CR-1 shall be achieved by fulfilling the following objectives:
1. Archive and index all OTC25 datasets
2. Preprocess OTC25 datasets as required
3. Provide access to OTC25 datasets for use by ESA OTC students staff and co-research teams
